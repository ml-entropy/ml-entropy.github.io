The Deep Connection

Boltzmann Entropy (Physics)
$$S = k_B \ln \Omega$$

Measures the number of microscopic configurations (microstates) consistent with a macroscopic state.

Shannon Entropy (Information)
$$H = -\sum_i p_i \log p_i$$

Measures the average surprise or uncertainty in a random variable.

Historical Note

Shannon deliberately chose the name "entropy" for his information measure because of its mathematical similarity to thermodynamic entropy. John von Neumann allegedly told Shannon: "You should call it entropy... no one really knows what entropy is, so in a debate you will always have the advantage."

Why Study Physics for ML?

The connection between physics and ML goes deeper than analogy:

  • Energy-based models treat model outputs as "energies"
  • Boltzmann machines are named after the physicist
  • Simulated annealing mimics metallurgical processes
  • Free energy principles appear in variational inference
