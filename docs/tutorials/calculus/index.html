Why This Matters for ML

Neural networks are just compositions of functions. Backpropagation is just the chain rule applied systematically. Understanding calculus at a deep level demystifies the entire learning process.

Key formula: $\frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial W^{(l)}} = \frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial z^{(l)}} \cdot \frac{\partial z^{(l)}}{\partial W^{(l)}}$ (chain rule!)
